about us

This is to showcases and sells traditional Nigeria drums, while also promoting the rich cultural heritage of Africa drumming. This aims to provide a platform for drum makers, artist and enthisiasts to connect, share knowledge, and appreciate the beauty of Nigeria. Its objectives is to create the online drums and drumming accessories, promoting Nigerian drumming culture and Tradition, to provide a platform for drum makers and artist to showcase their work , to offer educational resources and workshop on drumming and drum making and to foster a community of drum enthiusiasts and supporter

1. Djembe: Originating from the Yoruba people, the djembe is a rope-tuned goblet drum. 2. Talking Drum: Also known as the "drum of the gods," the talking drum is a hourglass-shaped drum used for communication and storytelling. 3. Bata Drum: A double-headed drum used in Yoruba culture for ceremonial and ritual purposes. 4. Conga Drum: A single-headed drum originating from the Igbo people.

1. Communication: Drums were used for communication, conveying messages and news across communities. 2. Ceremonies and Rituals: Drums played a crucial role in traditional ceremonies, such as weddings, births, and funerals. 3. Music and Dance: Drums provided the rhythmic foundation for traditional Nigerian music and dance. 4. Storytelling: Drums were used to tell stories, pass down history, and share cultural values.
